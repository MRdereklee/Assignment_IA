Club Recruitment

Club Recruitment OPEN NOW
10 April 2019 | Author: Lee Seng Ken


As the APU's Chess Society is growing, we would like to recruit more people to join as our club member. Our club are divided in two groups, one is for the International Chess and the other group is for Chinese Chess or if you willing to spend more time, you can join both the chess group.

Although our club is new and is not as big as other club like Japanese Club or Chinese Orchestra Club, our club are still growing and one day will become as popular as other club. The purpose of this club is to learn how to play mainly Internation Chess and Chinese Chess. Joining this club can also be an entertainment or relaxation after a long day study and work.

What are you waiting for? Join us now!
