Attire and Equipment

Brief about attire and equipment on chess


Attire for both type of chess

Every player or contestor is required to fear a certain type of attire when either participating an chess event, competition or even praticing.

When attending a competition:

For men:

  • Trouse or jeans
  • Long-sleeve or shirt-sleeve
  • Socks,shoes or sneakers (no slippers)

For women:

  • Blouse, T-shirts
  • Trousers or jeans, skirts or dresses
  • boots, flats, mid or high-heel shoes, sneakers with sock

Additional :

  • A jacket, vest, sweater or scarf if needed
  • All pieces of clothing should be clean or crisp
  • Glasses or neck tiles can be worn






Equipment for both type of chess

Equipments are needed to either to help the player or even the game to move smoothly.

Here are some equipment that is needed when playing a game of chess:


  • The type of chess board (only for pratice game) unless it is provided
  • A chess clock
  • A pen or pencil (to record the movement of the pieces)
