Latest News
     APU Chess Junior Minor Championship has began

The APU Chess Junior Minor Championship started on April 16 at 10 a.m. and will include 16 of the grandmasters from other universities and a prize pool of RM20,000. The winner of the APU Chess Junior Minor Championship will also earn a direct invite to the APU Chess Junior Major Championship and with prize pool of RM50,000.






     APU Chinese Chess Competiton coming next month

The APU Chinese Chess Competition will be held in next month. The competition is held on May 20 at 9 am. Anyone that is age 10 and above are allow to join the competiton. Every player has to fight their way to become a winner. The prize pool of this competiton is a total of RM 15,000.






     Club recruitment OPEN NOW

As the APU's Chess Society is growing, we would like to recruit more people to join as our club member. Our club are divided in two groups, one is for the International Chess and the other group is for Chinese Chess or if you willing to spend more time, you can join both the chess group.
