APU Chinese Chess Competiton coming next month
13 April 2019 | Author: Wong Wei Lok


The APU Chinese Chess Competition will be held in next month. The competition is held on May 20 at 9 am. Anyone that is age 10 and above are allow to join the competiton. Every player has to fight their way to become a winner. The prize pool of this competiton is a total of RM 15,000.

To join the Chinese Chess Competition, everyone have to register with us through online or come to the campus. When coming to campus for registration, everyone must wear appropiate attire. The competiton will be organized by and held in Asia Pacific University (APU).

The event of the APU Chinese Chess Competition will be broadcast live on APU's Youtube Channel and will be hosted by the commentators, Lee Seng Ken, President of APU's Chess Society.

Each match will have the following rules and format:

The winner of each match will proceed to the next bracket. The match will be best of three. The players that lost will be sent to lower bracket. In low bracket, the players had the second chance to fight their way back to upper bracket to continue to compete for their rights to become the winner.

The top 3 winners will be qualified to join the APU Chinese Chess Major Championship in the coming future as the Major Championship is allowed for qualified players only. The prize pool of the APU Chinese Chess Competiton is RM15,000.

For more information, you can send us enquiries or visit our campus at Techonology Park, Bukit Jalil.
