About Us

A brief introduction about us, our goals and contact information


About Us


Our club is an organisation of individuals who enjoy playing both Chinese and international chess as a source of enjoyment. Our club was founded back in 2019 when our club president (Derek Lee Seng Ken), vice president (Wong Wei Lok), Secretary (Soo Kok Thong) enjoy spending their free time playing international and Chinese chess and wished to find more students who shares the same interests to play chess with them. Hence, our club is registered in Asia Pacific University and manage to attract many students who shares the same interest as us to join our club to make our chess community bigger.



Our Goals


Vision


We dreamed of creating a world where teenagers are not obsessed with computer games

Mission


We wanted to spread the culture and encourage the hobby of playing international and Chinese chess to all the student in Asia Pacific University so that the student in Asia Pacific University can still have some enjoyment even without their electronic gadgets

Objectives


We aimed to be having at least 50 members for the club, we also hoped that students in Asia Pacific University can started to find joy in playing international and Chinese chess and we would be seeing the scene where students spend their free times by playing chess instead of their electronic gadgets
